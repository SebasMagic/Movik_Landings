Example

Say a carrier moves a $10,000 invoice and chooses to get paid same day. On the fee Movik collects on that load, you take 20% as your revenue share. And that repeats load after load — without you lifting a finger.

The revenue share is paid monthly, within 15 business days after month-end close, by ACH or check. Only once Movik has collected the fee, and only with carriers new to QuickPay.
